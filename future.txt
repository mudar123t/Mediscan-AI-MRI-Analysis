Evaluation methods:

1. Hausdorff Distance (advanced)
Measures boundary distance between predicted and GT masks.
Sensitive to shape errors
Harder to implement and interpret


more methods to study 

| Metric                | Measures                      | Why it’s advanced      |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Hausdorff / HD95      | Worst boundary error          | Catches shape failures |
| ASD                   | Mean boundary error           | Stable geometry metric |
| Volumetric Similarity | Tumor size error              | Clinically relevant    |
| ECE (Calibration)     | Confidence reliability        | Safety-critical        |
